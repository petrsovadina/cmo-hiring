# Competitive Intelligence Report: Smartsupp vs. Tidio

> **Report Date**: April 2026
> **Prepared by**: CI System (AI-assisted competitive analysis)

> **Sources**: Public websites, pricing pages, G2/Capterra reviews, blog content, social media, job postings

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Pricing Advantage** — Smartsupp's entry paid tier (€19.50/mo) significantly undercuts Tidio's (€29/mo), creating a 33% price advantage for SMB buyers. Tidio's steep jump to €59/mo (Growth) and €749/mo (Tidio+) creates friction points Smartsupp can exploit in competitive positioning.
- **AI Arms Race** — Tidio's Lyro AI is more aggressively marketed and positioned as a standalone product. Smartsupp's Mira AI is capable but under-promoted. Recommendation: elevate AI chatbot messaging to match market expectations.
- **Content Gap** — Tidio publishes 8-12 blog posts/month vs. Smartsupp's 2-4. Tidio dominates organic search for high-intent keywords like "live chat for ecommerce" and "chatbot for shopify." This is Smartsupp's biggest competitive weakness.
- **Unique Moat** — Smartsupp's video recordings remain a genuine differentiator with zero equivalent in Tidio's stack. This feature is under-leveraged in positioning and sales enablement.
- **Market Split** — Smartsupp dominates Central/Eastern Europe; Tidio is stronger in North America and Western Europe. Both are fighting over the global SMB e-commerce segment.

## 3. Pricing Intelligence

### Side-by-Side Comparison

| | Smartsupp | Tidio |
|---|----------|-------|
| **Free** | 1 agent, basic live chat, limited chatbot | 50 conversations/mo, basic features |
| **Tier 1** | Standard — €19.50/mo (3 agents) | Starter — €29/mo (100 conversations) |
| **Tier 2** | Pro — €48/mo (3 agents, full features) | Growth — €59/mo (up to 2,000 conversations) |
| **Tier 3** | Ultimate — custom pricing | Tidio+ — from €749/mo |
| **AI add-on** | Included in tiers | Lyro AI — separate pricing, per conversation |

### Pricing Model Differences

**Smartsupp**: Agent-based pricing (pay per seat)
- Predictable costs
- Scales with team size
- Conversations unlimited within plan

**Tidio**: Conversation-based pricing (pay per interaction)
- Can become expensive at volume
- Unpredictable for growing businesses
- AI conversations cost extra

### Segment Analysis

| Buyer Segment | Better Value | Why |
|--------------|-------------|-----|
| Solopreneur (1 agent, <100 chats/mo) | Tie | Both free tiers work |
| Small shop (1-3 agents, 200-500 chats/mo) | **Smartsupp** | €19.50 vs €29-59 at Tidio |
| Growing SMB (3-5 agents, 1000+ chats/mo) | **Smartsupp** | Tidio's Growth tier caps at 2000 |
| Mid-market (10+ agents, 5000+ chats/mo) | Depends | Both move to custom/enterprise |

### Pricing Vulnerabilities

**Tidio's Weaknesses**:
- Steep jump from free (50 convos) to paid (€29) — creates churn risk
- AI conversations priced separately — total cost unclear
- Tidio+ at €749/mo is a massive jump — mid-market gap

**Smartsupp's Weaknesses**:
- Agent-based pricing less favorable for large teams
- No email marketing included (Tidio bundles this)
- Perceived as "cheaper = less powerful"

### Recommendations
1. **Exploit Tidio's pricing opacity** — Create a "true cost calculator" landing page showing what Tidio really costs with AI conversations
2. **Emphasize unlimited conversations** — This is a major advantage for volume-driven businesses
3. **Create a "Switch from Tidio" offer** — Target Tidio users hitting conversation limits with a migration incentive
