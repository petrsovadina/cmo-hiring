Monitor competitor content strategy — blog output, SEO rankings, content topics, and organic search positioning.

## Collection Method

### Blog Content (Weekly)
1. Visit blog RSS feed or main blog page
2. Count new posts since last check
3. Categorize by topic:
   - Product updates / changelog
   - How-to / tutorial
   - Industry / thought leadership
   - Comparison / vs. competitor
   - Case study / customer story
   - SEO-optimized landing page

### SEO Monitoring (Monthly)
```
Tools (free tier):
- Ubersuggest: domain overview, top pages
- Google: site:tidio.com "vs" OR "alternative" (comparison pages)
- Google: site:tidio.com/blog (indexed blog posts)
- Ahrefs free backlink checker
```

## AI Analysis Prompt

```
You are an SEO and content strategy analyst. Compare these content outputs:

SMARTSUPP CONTENT (last 30 days):
- Blog posts: {{smartsupp_posts}}
- Topics: {{smartsupp_topics}}
- New pages: {{smartsupp_new_pages}}

TIDIO CONTENT (last 30 days):
- Blog posts: {{tidio_posts}}
- Topics: {{tidio_topics}}
- New pages: {{tidio_new_pages}}

Analyze:
1. CONTENT VELOCITY — who's publishing more and in which categories?
2. TOPIC FOCUS — what themes is each company investing in?
3. SEO STRATEGY — what keywords are they targeting?
4. COMPARISON CONTENT — are they creating "vs" pages? Against whom?
5. CONTENT GAPS — what topics is Tidio covering that Smartsupp isn't?
6. RECOMMENDATIONS — top 5 content pieces Smartsupp should create based on gaps
```
