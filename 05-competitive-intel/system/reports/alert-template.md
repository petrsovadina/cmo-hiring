# Competitive Alert Template

## For urgent competitive signals that can't wait for the weekly report.

```
================================================================
🚨 COMPETITIVE ALERT — {{urgency_level}}
================================================================

Date: {{date}}
Competitor: Tidio
Category: {{category}}

WHAT HAPPENED:
{{description}}

WHY IT MATTERS:
{{impact_analysis}}

EVIDENCE:
- Source: {{url}}
- Screenshot: {{attachment}}
- Additional context: {{context}}

RECOMMENDED RESPONSE:
{{recommended_actions}}

OWNER: {{suggested_owner}}
DEADLINE: {{response_deadline}}

================================================================
```

## Distribution Rules

| Urgency | Channel | Recipients |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| CRITICAL | Slack DM + #competitive-intel + email | CMO, VP Product, VP Sales |
| HIGH | #competitive-intel | Marketing team, Product leads |
| MEDIUM | Weekly report only | Marketing team |
