## When We Win

1. **Price-sensitive buyers** — Our paid plans start lower (€19.50 vs €29)
2. **E-commerce analytics** — Video recordings show real visitor behavior
3. **European market** — Strong CZ/SK/PL/DACH presence, EU data compliance
4. **Simplicity** — Faster setup, less overwhelming for small teams

**Talk track**: "Smartsupp gives you everything you need to convert visitors into buyers — live chat, AI chatbot, and unique visitor insights like video recordings — at a price point that grows with your business."

## When We Lose

1. **Shopify-heavy stores** — Tidio's Shopify integration is deeper
2. **AI chatbot sophistication** — Lyro AI has more advanced NLP
3. **Email marketing combo** — Tidio bundles email, we don't
4. **Brand awareness (US)** — Tidio is better known in North America

## Common Objections

### "Tidio has better AI chatbots"
> "Tidio's Lyro AI is strong, but look at the pricing — AI conversations cost extra and add up fast. Smartsupp's AI chatbot handles the most common e-commerce scenarios out of the box, and our video recordings give you insights no chatbot can — you see exactly where customers struggle."

### "Tidio is cheaper"
> "At first glance, maybe — but compare apples to apples. Tidio's free plan limits you to 50 conversations/month. Their Growth plan at €29/mo still limits Lyro AI conversations. With Smartsupp, you get more value at each tier without surprise caps."

### "Everyone uses Tidio"
> "Tidio is popular in North America, but in Europe — where your customers are — Smartsupp is the market leader. We serve 100,000+ active businesses, with local support and EU data compliance built in."

## Pricing Quick Reference

### Smartsupp
- **Free**: 1 agent, basic chat
- **Standard** (€19.50/mo): 3 agents, chatbots
- **Pro** (€48/mo): 3 agents, advanced features
- **Ultimate**: Custom pricing

### Tidio

This is our unique moat. No competitor in the live chat space offers this.
