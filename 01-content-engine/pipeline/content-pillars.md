# Abugo Content Pillars

## Strategic Positioning

**Mission:** Position Abugo as the leading European commerce SaaS holding -- a builder, not just an acquirer. The narrative: "We don't just buy companies. We build an ecosystem where commerce tools make each other better."

**Target Audiences:**
- SaaS founders considering exit / partnership (potential acquisitions)
- Commerce professionals (e-commerce managers, marketers, ops leads)
- Tech talent in CEE (recruiting pipeline)
- Investors and industry analysts

---

## Pillar 1: Building a Commerce Ecosystem

**Theme:** How Abugo's portfolio companies create synergies and compound value together.

**Topics:**
- Cross-product integrations (Smartsupp chat on Shopsys stores, Reservio + Survio feedback loops)
- Shared infrastructure and learnings across 7 companies
- The "holding as platform" model vs. traditional PE roll-ups
- Why vertical SaaS beats horizontal in commerce

**Angle:** Behind-the-scenes builder stories. Show the craft of running 7 products as one ecosystem.

**Frequency:** 2x/week

---

## Pillar 2: European SaaS Playbook

**Theme:** Insights on building and scaling SaaS from Central/Eastern Europe.

**Topics:**
- Bootstrapping vs. VC in CEE markets
- Hiring and retaining tech talent in Brno/CZ
- Competing globally from a European base (compliance as advantage, not burden)
- Abugo's journey: from single product to 7-company holding
- GDPR and EU compliance as competitive moat

**Angle:** Practical lessons with numbers. Not cheerleading -- real operator insights.

**Frequency:** 2x/week

---

## Pillar 3: Commerce Automation & AI

**Theme:** How AI and automation are transforming online commerce -- through the lens of Abugo's products.

**Topics:**
- AI chatbots in e-commerce (Smartsupp's Mira AI)
- Automated marketing for e-shops (Tanganica)
- Checkout optimization with data (Convertim)
- The future of headless commerce (Shopsys)
- AI-powered booking management (Reservio)

**Angle:** Product-led content that educates without being salesy. "Here's what we're seeing" not "buy our product."

**Frequency:** 2x/week

---

## Pillar 4: Customer Success Stories

**Theme:** Real results from real businesses using Abugo portfolio products.

**Topics:**
- SMB booking transformation stories (Reservio -- 20M+ bookings/year)
- E-commerce conversion wins (Smartsupp, Convertim)
- Survey-driven business decisions (Survio)
- Enterprise e-commerce migrations (Shopsys)
- Shopify growth stories (Sounds Good Agency)

**Angle:** Data-driven case studies. Lead with the number, tell the story.

**Frequency:** 1x/week

---

## Pillar 5: Team & Culture

**Theme:** What it's like to work at Abugo and its companies. Employer branding + authenticity.

**Topics:**
- Engineering culture across 7 companies
- Remote/hybrid work in a multi-company holding
- Career paths within the Abugo ecosystem (move between companies)
- Team events, hackathons, community involvement in Brno
- Founder stories and leadership perspectives

**Angle:** Authentic, people-first. No corporate-speak. Real people, real stories.

**Frequency:** 1x/week
