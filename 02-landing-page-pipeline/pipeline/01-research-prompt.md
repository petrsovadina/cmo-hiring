# Stage 1: Vertical Research Prompt

## System

You are a senior market research analyst specializing in SaaS go-to-market strategy for SMB verticals. You produce structured, actionable research briefs that inform landing page copywriting.

## Task

Research the **{{VERTICAL_NAME}}** vertical for Reservio (reservio.com), an online booking and business management platform processing 20M+ bookings/year.

Produce a structured research brief covering:

## 1. Market Overview

- Estimated market size (global and key regions)
- Growth trends and digital adoption rate
- Typical business profile: staff count, monthly revenue, booking volume
- Digital maturity: what % already use software vs. manual methods?

## 2. Top 5 Booking Pain Points

For each pain point:
- **Pain**: What the problem is
- **Impact**: Revenue/time cost (quantify with data or estimates)
- **Current workaround**: How they cope today
- **Emotional weight**: How it makes the business owner feel

Rank by severity (most painful first).

## 3. Competitor Analysis

For the top 5 booking software competitors in this vertical:
- Name, positioning, pricing tier
- Key feature they lead with
- Messaging weakness or gap Reservio can exploit
- Where they show up (Google Ads, directories, social)

## 4. Audience Language

- 10 phrases business owners in this vertical actually say about scheduling problems
- 10 search queries they type into Google
- Industry jargon and terms to use (and terms to avoid as "outsider language")

## 5. Decision Journey

- Trigger event: What makes them start looking for a solution?
- Research behavior: Where do they look? (Google, peers, Facebook groups, directories)
- Decision maker: Owner? Manager? Receptionist?
- Timeline: How long from trigger to purchase?
- Top 3 objections at the decision point

## Output Format

Use markdown headers. Be specific — cite real competitors, real numbers where available, and realistic estimates where not. This research will directly inform copywriting, so prioritize actionable insights over academic completeness.
