## FAQ SECTION

**Q: Can my clients really book both group classes and personal training sessions?**

Yes. Reservio handles both seamlessly. Set up group classes with capacity limits and waitlists, and offer one-on-one personal training slots — all from the same booking page. Your clients see what's available and book instantly.

---

**Q: I'm not tech-savvy at all. How hard is this to set up?**

If you can post on Instagram, you can set up Reservio. Add your services, set your hours, customize your page — most studios are fully live within 15 minutes. We also have step-by-step video guides and a support chat if you get stuck at any point.

---

**Q: How much does it cost?**

Reservio has a free plan that covers core booking features — and it's free forever, not a trial. Paid plans start at around €10/month and add features like automated SMS reminders, online payments, and unlimited booking history. Most trainers start free and upgrade once they see the results.

---

**Q: Will it actually help with no-shows?**

Significantly. Automated email and SMS reminders are sent before each session — your clients confirm or reschedule with one tap. Studios typically see a 40-50% drop in no-shows within the first month. That's hundreds of euros back in your pocket.

---

**Q: Can I accept payments online?**

Yes. Clients can pay at the time of booking using credit card, debit card, or other local payment methods. You can also sell session packages (e.g., 10 sessions for €450) and memberships, with Reservio automatically tracking remaining sessions.

---

**Q: I already have a website. Does Reservio replace it?**

No — Reservio works alongside your website. Embed the booking widget directly on your site, or add a simple "Book Now" button that links to your Reservio page. It also integrates with your Google Business profile, Facebook page, and Instagram bio.
