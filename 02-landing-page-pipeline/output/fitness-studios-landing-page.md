## STAGE 1 OUTPUT: VERTICAL RESEARCH

### 1. Market Overview

- **Global fitness market**: $96B+ (2024), projected $135B by 2028
- **Studio/gym count**: ~200,000 fitness studios in Europe, ~40,000 in US alone
- **Typical profile**: 1-5 staff, 100-500 active clients, €3,000-15,000/month revenue
- **Digital maturity**: ~40% still use paper/phone/WhatsApp for bookings. ~35% use basic software (Calendly, Google Calendar). ~25% use dedicated fitness booking platforms
- **Key trend**: Post-COVID shift to hybrid (in-studio + online classes) created urgent need for proper booking systems

### 2. Top 5 Booking Pain Points

| # | Pain | Impact | Current Workaround | Emotional Weight |
|---|------|--------|--------------------|------------------|
| 1 | **No-shows and late cancellations** | 15-25% revenue loss; average studio loses €500-2,000/month | Manual text reminders, penalty policies nobody enforces | Frustration, feeling disrespected |
| 2 | **After-hours booking requests** | Missed revenue; 60% of booking intent happens outside business hours (evenings, weekends) | "DM me on Instagram" or "Call tomorrow" | Anxiety about lost clients |
| 3 | **Manual scheduling chaos** | 5-10 hours/week on admin; double-bookings, calendar conflicts | Paper diary, WhatsApp threads, Excel sheets | Overwhelm, burnout |
| 4 | **No client history or insights** | Can't personalize, can't track attendance trends, can't identify churn risk | Memory, sticky notes, basic spreadsheets | Flying blind |
| 5 | **Payment friction** | Delayed payments, cash-only limits, package tracking headaches | Bank transfers, Venmo/Revolut, manual counting | Awkward money conversations |

### 3. Competitor Analysis

| Competitor | Positioning | Pricing | Lead Feature | Messaging Gap |
|-----------|-------------|---------|--------------|---------------|
| **Mindbody** | Enterprise fitness platform | $139-699/month | All-in-one for large gyms | Overkill and overpriced for small studios; complex onboarding |
| **Acuity (Squarespace)** | General scheduling | $16-49/month | Calendar integration | Not fitness-specific; no class booking, no group sessions |
| **Fitogram** | Boutique fitness studios | €29-99/month | Class management | Limited outside DACH region; weak payment processing |
| **Bookee** | Fitness & wellness | Custom pricing | Mobile-first booking | No free plan; opaque pricing scares small operators |
| **Calendly** | Generic scheduling | $8-16/month | Simple 1-on-1 booking | No group classes, no client management, no payments |

**Reservio opportunity**: Affordable full-featured alternative positioned between "too simple" (Calendly/Acuity) and "too expensive/complex" (Mindbody). Free plan as entry point.

### 5. Decision Journey

- **Trigger**: Lost a client to a competitor with online booking, OR had 3+ no-shows in one week, OR spent a Sunday evening rescheduling Monday's calendar
- **Research**: Google search → competitor websites → Facebook groups ("What booking system do you use?") → Capterra/G2 reviews → free trial
- **Decision maker**: Owner (90% of small studios), sometimes front desk manager
- **Timeline**: 1-3 weeks from trigger to trial signup; 1-2 weeks trial before purchase
- **Top 3 objections**: (1) "Will my clients actually use it?" (2) "I'm not technical enough" (3) "I can't afford another monthly bill"
